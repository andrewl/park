Save Staines Park - Once It's Gone, It's Gone.

Latest News:

We welcome the news that the council have officially said they are now considering other locations, we are relieved that they are listening to residents finally, while this is good news, they have NOT ruled out Staines Park as of yet and we will be pushing for it to be excluded and furthermore protected going forward.

Spelthorne Council want to develop on Staines Park (aka Commercial Road Park). Their plans to build a huge privately-run leisure centre complex on Staines Park will mean the loss of trees, parkland, free-to-use tennis courts, basketball courts and the bowls club.

The council's consultation exercise, run by a taxpayer-funded PR company from outside of the borough, did not address the issue of location.

Save Staines Park for families, for people of all ages, for the fit and less able, for good physical and mental wellbeing, for a better environment, a place for all the community now and for future generations.
