Save Our Park

Staines Park is under threat from development by Spelthorne Borough Council.

What's Happening?

Staines Park is under threat from development by Spelthorne Borough Council. A proposed huge expansion of the leisure centre threatens to destroy a large part of Staines Park. Although claiming to consult widely, the council are only seeking opinions on what facilities the new centre should provide, but aren't asking whether we want to lose most of our park. You can find the council's consultation document at http://spelthorneleisure.info/Spelthorne_Consultation_Boards.pdf.
