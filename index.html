Save Staines Park

Staines Park - Saved!

We are very pleased that Spelthorne Council have recognised the passion local residents have about Staines Park and Staines Bowling Club to remove it from the scope of the Leisure Centre development.

Read Spelthorne Borough Council's press release here

John Want, spokesman for the Group said, "We are over the moon with this result. It is a reflection of the effort my colleagues and I have put in to raise awareness of the plans and local residents in their communications to councillors rejecting the proposals. However whilst this is indeed democracy in action as Councillor Harvey states, if Spelthorne Council had carried out the consultation properly, in an open and honest manner, we would have got to this point earlier and there would been less waste of public funds." Save Staines Park Group will remain active as it seeks its ultimate aim of protecting the park and the bowling club for future generations. Want continues, "Our Group will continue to press the council for officially protecting the park, not just from Leisure Centre development, but from all development. We've discovered a fantastic mix of wildlife in the park, a number of mature and some rare trees and of course a community who value the park more than our Council thought. We will also keep up the pressure on the Leisure Centre plans to ensure any future consultation is carried out properly. This is indeed wonderful news but our work continues."
