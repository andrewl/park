Save Staines Park

Staines Park (aka Commercial Road Park) is under threat from development by Spelthorne Borough Council.

Act Now - Time is Running Out!

Spelthorne Council want to develop on Staines Park (aka Commercial Road Park), removing around half of the park to replace it with fenced-off, pay-per-use plastic football pitches. If you do just one thing, please fill out the council's survey. It takes just 2 minutes. You can find it here. Two minutes and you can help save our park! Questions 10 and 13 are where you can tell the council that you object to development on the park.
